Executive Summary

MRIoA is evaluating Outreach to replace Salesforce Engage (expires mid-2026) and consolidate our fragmented sales technology stack. This evaluation was initiated proactively, recognizing that our current tool ecosystem has created significant workflow inefficiencies across Business Development, Sales, and Customer Success teams. Over the course of four discovery and demo sessions, the evaluation team conducted a thorough assessment of how Outreach addresses the specific challenges we face today.

The evaluation process revealed a clear pattern: our teams are spending disproportionate amounts of time on administrative tasks, manual research, and navigating between disconnected systems. BD reps spend 10 minutes preparing for each call and navigate 15 clicks in Salesforce just to log basic activity. CS managers invest hours compiling account intelligence from multiple sources before QBRs. Sales teams lack visibility into what other teams are doing, leading to duplicate outreach and missed handoff opportunities. These inefficiencies compound daily, costing hours per rep per week that could be dedicated to actual selling and strategic account work.

What emerged from our evaluation is that Outreach isn't just a replacement for Salesforce Engage—it's a unified platform that addresses workflow inefficiencies across all three teams while providing the trusted metrics and visibility that leadership needs. The platform consolidates functionality that's currently scattered across multiple tools, automates manual processes that consume rep time, and creates the cross-functional visibility that's been missing. Most importantly, it provides the systematic approach to renewal management, lead prioritization, and account intelligence that will enable our 2026 strategic goals around white space expansion.

Bottom Line

Outreach addresses critical workflow inefficiencies across BD, Sales, and CS teams while providing the trusted metrics and visibility leadership needs. The evaluation demonstrates clear ROI through time savings, tool consolidation, and systematic automation that will enable our teams to focus on what matters: building relationships, closing deals, and expanding accounts.

Problem 1: Manual, Time-Consuming Workflows

Current State

  • 10 minutes to prepare for a single call (per Angela's documented process)
  • 15 clicks in Salesforce to log activity, update fields, mark invalid numbers
  • Manual compilation of reports for board meetings
  • Hours spent researching accounts before QBRs (per Sarah's team)

Business Impact

  • Reps spend more time on admin than selling
  • "Pencil whipping" data entry to get through it (quality suffers)
  • Inconsistent call documentation ("good meeting" notes with no detail)
"It takes 10 minutes just to get ready to make a call." — Breanna

Problem 2: Team Silos with Zero Cross-Functional Visibility

Current State

  • BD, Sales, and CS work in isolation
  • No visibility into who contacted whom, when, about what
  • Duplicate outreach to same contacts by different teams
  • Missed handoff opportunities when transitioning accounts

Business Impact

  • Confused prospects receiving multiple calls from different teams
  • Lost context when CS takes over from Sales (have to ask rep to recap)
  • Wasted effort on contacts already being worked
"We want to say, 'Lori just talked with this person 2 days ago—I don't need to reach out.'" — Breanna

Problem 3: Zero Renewal Automation

Current State

  • No proactive triggers at 120/90/60/30 days before renewal
  • No Salesforce reports tracking upcoming contract expirations
  • Evergreen contracts mean renewals "sneak up" on CS team
  • Reactive instead of proactive account management

Business Impact

  • Missed expansion opportunities
  • Risk of churn due to lack of early intervention
  • CS team unable to systematically manage white space expansion (2026 strategic goal)
"There is nothing that I know of that has a list of all clients and all of their contract expiration dates." — Lori
"We don't have anything that I know of for [at-risk account tracking] either." — Lori

Business Impact

Solution 1: Streamlined Task Execution

What Outreach Does

  • 15 clicks → 2 clicks to complete call, log activity, book meeting
  • 10 minutes → 2 minutes to prep for and execute a call
  • Integrated dialer with live calendar booking
  • Automatic activity logging (no manual entry ever again)
  • Mark invalid numbers once, visible to all teams
  • Queue up 50 tasks, fly through in 30 minutes vs. hours

Impact

  • 8-10 minutes saved per call × call volume = hours back per rep per week
  • Higher data quality (no more "pencil whipping" entries)
  • No more "good meeting" notes—automatic call recording and transcription
"Add that up with whatever their call allotment is for the week, that's going to significantly impact." — Breanna

Solution 2: Smart Account Plans - Unified Cross-Team Visibility

What Outreach Does

  • 360° account view showing ALL activity from BD, Sales, CS in one timeline
  • AI summaries: Ask "what matters to this account?" and get instant answers
  • Custom views by team (CS: renewals in 90 days, BD: closed-lost opps)
  • Activity heatmaps showing engagement patterns

Impact

  • Eliminate duplicate outreach (see "Lori called yesterday")
  • Seamless handoffs with full context
  • Managers can audit any account in seconds, not hours
"This puts everything you have to go into one thing." — Lori

Solution 3: Trigger-Based Renewal Automation

What Outreach Does

  • Read renewal date field in Salesforce
  • Automatically trigger sequences at 120/90/60/30 days before renewal
  • QBR scheduling automation
  • Usage data triggers (if Power BI metrics pushed to Salesforce)

Impact

  • Systematic renewal management (no contracts sneaking up)
  • Proactive CS instead of reactive firefighting
  • Enables 2026 white space expansion strategy

Solution 4: Intelligent Lead Routing & Prioritization

What Outreach Does

  • Automation triggers by lead source + title: Demo request (exec) → high-touch sequence with calendar link in 2 minutes
  • White paper download → automated nurture
  • Smart Views filter by engagement, priority, lead score
  • Multi-channel sequences (email → call → LinkedIn → SMS)

Impact

  • High-value leads get immediate attention
  • Low-value leads get touched without wasting rep time
  • Measurable conversion rates by sequence/campaign

Solution 5: AI-Powered Account Research

What Outreach Does

  • Gemini AI research agent auto-enriches accounts with external data (news, funding, leadership changes)
  • AI account summaries from all emails, calls, meetings ("What pain points has this account mentioned?")
  • Runs on schedule or on-demand

Impact

  • Hours → minutes for account prep
  • Consistent intelligence across team
  • Scalable CS approach
"Two years ago our CS manager showed all of the data you could get... she said it takes hours." — Breanna

Solution 6: Trusted Reporting & Analytics

What Outreach Does

  • Sequence performance with pipeline attribution (which campaigns drive revenue)
  • Persona filtering using ML (auto-groups "Chief Compliance Officer" into C-Suite)
  • User activity dashboards (prospects added, calls made, overdue tasks)
  • Email sentiment analysis (track objections to re-engage systematically)
  • Customizable reports for board meetings (no more manual compilation)

Impact

  • Data-driven optimization of messaging and campaigns
  • ROI justification for marketing/sales investments
  • Executive visibility without manual work
"This basically solves all of our metrics." — Chris
"This answers what we were talking about this morning." — Breanna (regarding tracking BD activity properly)

What Outreach Replaces/Adds
